🔒 Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 11, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to FinTrack Cloud. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use FinTrack Cloud:

  • Account Information: Email address and password (encrypted)
  • Transaction Data: Amounts, categories, payment methods, dates, and descriptions you enter
  • Payment Methods: Custom payment method names you create
  • Usage Data: Last report date for WhatsApp reporting feature

3. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely for:

  • Providing the expense tracking service
  • Generating weekly financial reports
  • Syncing your data across devices
  • Maintaining and improving our service

4. Data Storage & Security

All data is stored securely using Google Firebase:

  • Authentication: Firebase Authentication with industry-standard security
  • Database: Cloud Firestore with server-side security rules
  • Encryption: Data encrypted in transit (HTTPS) and at rest
  • Access Control: Only you can access your own data

5. Data Sharing

We do NOT:

  • Sell your data to third parties
  • Share your financial information with advertisers
  • Use your data for marketing purposes

Your data is stored on Firebase servers (Google Cloud Platform) as required to provide the service.

6. WhatsApp Integration

Our WhatsApp reporting feature:

  • Operates client-side only (no server sends messages)
  • Opens WhatsApp with a pre-filled message only when you click "Send"
  • Does not store your phone number
  • Does not have access to your WhatsApp account

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data at any time through the app
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Export your transaction data
  • Opt out of WhatsApp reporting (simply don't use the feature)

8. Cookies & Tracking

FinTrack Cloud uses:

  • LocalStorage: To cache WhatsApp report status (no personal data)
  • Firebase Auth Tokens: To maintain your login session

We do NOT use advertising cookies or third-party trackers.

9. Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. Upon account deletion, all data is permanently removed from our systems.

10. Children's Privacy

FinTrack Cloud is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

12. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at:

Email: support@fintrackcloud.app

13. GDPR Compliance

For users in the European Union:

  • We process data lawfully based on your consent
  • You have the right to data portability
  • You can request data deletion at any time
  • We comply with GDPR data protection requirements
